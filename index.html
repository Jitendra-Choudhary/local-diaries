Meet Neelam

I chase the moments most people miss—raw street energy, fleeting colors in the sky, and details that vanish as fast as they appear. Every picture speaks its own story, and I try to catch them in their most honest form. My photography is spontaneous, unfiltered, and rooted in curiosity.

The Camera Behind My Stories

I shoot with the Fujifilm X-M5, a camera that feels like an extension of my eye. Its colors, portability, and character help me capture moments as they naturally unfold—nothing forced, just real scenes told through a lens that I trust.
